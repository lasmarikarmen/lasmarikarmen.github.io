ARTISTS

Las Marikarmen is a transvestite, transfeminist and queer band that mixes music, performance, drag, and experiences. We make our dissident identities visible on stage where we all sing, play, perform, dance, launch videos... whatever it takes! We make urban music with the the brass band punch of saxophones to make the audience vibrate, and we invite you to enter our world of fantasy and glitter.
The band's success is based on the participation of many different people since 2017.
